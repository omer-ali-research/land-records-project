County maps

One map per county that has geocoded rows in the summary. The view is limited to the county boundary (Census outline); areas outside are dimmed. Only points with a parseable year in 1935–1975 and classified as FHA or VA are shown (same rules as the Mortgages tables). Markers are fixed on the map; changing the Period dropdown only filters which points are visible. All years colors markers by calendar year. FHA uses × markers; VA uses circles. There is no clustering.
